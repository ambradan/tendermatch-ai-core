# Technical Roadmap

This document outlines technical directions for TenderMatch.
It is non-binding and subject to revision based on market feedback and operational priorities.

---

## Strategic Context

TenderMatch is evolving from a single-tenant compliance tool toward an enterprise-grade
AI compliance platform. The roadmap reflects this trajectory.

---

## Near-Term (Q1–Q2 2025)

**Focus: Auditability & Trust**

| Initiative | Rationale |
|------------|-----------|
| Compliance explainability layer | Enterprise buyers require traceable AI decisions |
| Extended audit logging | Regulatory and procurement audit requirements |
| Scorecard versioning (v2) | Backward compatibility for long-running evaluations |

---

## Mid-Term (Q3–Q4 2025)

**Focus: Scale & Multi-Tenancy**

| Initiative | Rationale |
|------------|-----------|
| Multi-tenant isolation | Enable B2B SaaS model with data segregation |
| Role-based access controls | Enterprise permission requirements |
| Performance optimization | Support for large tender document sets (100+ pages) |

---

## Under Evaluation

| Initiative | Dependency |
|------------|------------|
| Additional document formats (DOCX, scanned PDF) | OCR provider selection |
| External procurement system integrations | Customer demand validation |
| On-premise deployment option | Enterprise security requirements |

---

## Non-Goals (Current Phase)

- White-label or reseller model
- Consumer-facing features
- Real-time collaboration

---

## Governance

Roadmap is reviewed quarterly by the product and engineering team.
External commitments are not made based on this document.
